Recipes Using Meadowgold Products

Meadowgold Cheddar Mac & Cheese

  • 2 cups Meadowgold Cheddar Cheese, shredded
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions: Cook macaroni. Melt butter, stir in flour, then milk. Add cheese, stir until melted. Combine with macaroni, season, and serve.

Meadowgold Yogurt Parfait

  • 1 cup Meadowgold Yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup fresh berries
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions: Layer yogurt, granola, and berries in a glass. Drizzle with honey and enjoy.

Meadowgold Butter Biscuits

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup Meadowgold Butter, cold
  • 3/4 cup milk

Instructions: Mix dry ingredients. Cut in butter. Add milk, mix to form dough. Cut biscuits, bake at 425Â°F for 12-15 min.

Meadowgold Creamy Potato Soup

  • 4 cups potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup Meadpwgold Cream
  • 1/2 cup Meadowgold Milk
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions: Cook potatoes and onion in butter. Add milk and cream, simmer until thick. Season and serve hot.
